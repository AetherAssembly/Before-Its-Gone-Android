First beta release of Before It's Gone for Android.

• Inventory tracking with expiry dates, barcode scanning, and low-stock alerts
• Shopping list auto-generated from low-stock items
• Waste log with category breakdown
• Recipe suggestions for expiring ingredients via TheMealDB
• Daily expiry notifications via WorkManager
• Optional Supabase cloud sync, compatible with the desktop app
• JSON and CSV import/export
• Material You dynamic color, dark/light theme
• Two flavors: ML Kit (Play) and ZXing (F-Droid/IzzyOnDroid)
