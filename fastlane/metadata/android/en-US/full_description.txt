Before It's Gone helps you track what's in your fridge, freezer, and pantry — and alerts you before things expire.

FEATURES
• Inventory tracking across fridge, freezer, and pantry (with custom locations)
• Expiry date alerts via Android notifications — no account required
• Barcode scanning to look up product names and shelf-life data from Open Food Facts
• Shopping list based on low-stock items
• Waste log to track what you threw away and why
• Recipe suggestions when items are about to expire (powered by TheMealDB)
• Optional Supabase cloud sync to share inventory across devices
• JSON and CSV import/export, fully compatible with the Before It's Gone desktop app
• Dark and light theme, Material You dynamic color

PRIVACY FIRST
All data is stored on your device in a local Room database. The camera is used only for barcode scanning and photo capture — frames are processed on-device and never transmitted. Barcode lookups contact Open Food Facts only when you trigger them. Notifications are generated locally. No tracking, no ads, no accounts required.

OPEN SOURCE
Before It's Gone is free and open-source software, licensed under AGPL-3.0. Source code is available at github.com/AetherAssembly/Before-Its-Gone-Android.

TWO FLAVORS
• Google Play edition: uses ML Kit for fast on-device barcode scanning
• F-Droid / IzzyOnDroid edition: uses ZXing, fully free of Google services — same features, no GMS dependency
